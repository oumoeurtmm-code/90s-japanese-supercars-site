Top 5 '90s Japanese Supercars

Relive the golden era of JDM performance!

1. Nissan Skyline GT-R R34

The ultimate "Godzilla" with ATTESA all-wheel drive and a tunable RB26DETT engine.

2. Toyota Supra MkIV Turbo

Powered by the legendary 2JZ-GTE, a tuner favorite with over 1,000 hp potential.

3. Mazda RX-7 FD

Rotary Wankel engine delivers smooth revs up to 9,000 RPM in a balanced chassis.

4. Honda NSX-R

Mid-engine layout and aluminum body make it a precise, Ferrari-beating supercar.

5. Mitsubishi 3000GT

Twin-turbo V6 with active aerodynamics and four-wheel steering for grand touring thrills.
